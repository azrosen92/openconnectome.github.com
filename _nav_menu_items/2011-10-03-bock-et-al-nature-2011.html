Mouse V1 Layer 2/3 (Nature, 2011) Davi Bock, Ph.D. and Wei-Chung Allen Lee, Ph.D., in the laboratory of Clay Reid, M.D., Ph.D. acquired a beautiful volume of mouse primary visual cortical data, spanning layers 1, 2/3, and upper layer 4.  In addition to the electron microscope (EM) data, they used two-photon microscopy to determine the functional properties of about 14 of the cells in the same volume.   Specifications Organism Mouse Brain region Primary visual cortex layers 1, 2/3, and upper 4 Resolution 4x4x45 nm3 Total volume 450x350x50 μm3 Web viewing Yes Sample downloads Yes Volume cutout service Yes Recommended Software TrakEM2 Connection Matrix Available upon request Functional data Available upon request Annotations Not yet Disk space 12TB Reference Nature (2011), pdf, suppl.
